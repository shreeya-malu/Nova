Who are we?


We are a group of enthusiastic students united by our fascination with the universe and its mysteries. Driven by curiosity about what lies beyond the stars, we have come together to build and experiment with model rockets.
Our goal is to learn and deepen our understanding of space and rocket science through hands-on experience. By combining our talents and passion, we aim to explore these concepts in a practical, educational way.

Our Goals?


At NOVA, our goal is to design high-powered model rockets with successful payload ejection and recovery systems. We aim to master the fundamentals of rocket design while applying our engineering skills to develop innovative and efficient rockets. By combining theoretical knowledge with practical application, we strive to push the boundaries of rocket technology and achieve remarkable performance in our designs.

What's so Different about us compared to others?


What differentiates us from other teams is our disciplined approach, effective communication, and emphasis on observational learning.
At Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Pune, we are dedicated to represent our college at both national and international levels. Our rigorous methodology ensures that we excel in all aspects of our projects, while our collaborative communication fosters a cohesive and innovative environment.
