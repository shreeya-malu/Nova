About Us

Space is something that always makes us wonder “what’s beyond?”. Some deeply motivated space enthusiasts have formed this team in order to explore their interest and passion in space and Rocket science. NOVA is a team that aspires to design high powered model rockets, with a successful payload ejection and a recovery system


Our primary focus is to learn the fundamentals of rocket designing and incorporate it with our engineering skills in order to make Rockets from scratch. A disciplined approach, good communication and observational learning is a differentiator for us. We here at Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Pune have a mission of representing our college at national and international levels in these Rocketry Competitions.

We are on a journey to inspire, innovate and discover and we would love for you to be a part of it!
